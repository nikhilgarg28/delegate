Design Considerations

  • Variation 1 (0.6 opacity): Most pronounced differentiation. Boss messages clearly stand out. May make non-boss messages feel too de-emphasized.
  • Variation 2 (0.75 opacity + font-weight 300): More balanced approach. The lighter font weight adds a subtle texture difference that works well with the opacity. Recommended for better readability while maintaining clear hierarchy.
  • Variation 3 (indentation + 0.7 opacity): Uses spatial hierarchy in addition to opacity. Boss messages align to the left edge, creating a visual anchor. Non-boss messages are visually nested, which may help scanning but uses more horizontal space.
  • All variations: Removed background color and left border. Messages have uniform visual weight in terms of spatial presence, with differentiation coming from foreground treatment only.
  • Accessibility note: Variation 2 or 3 recommended over Variation 1 for better contrast ratios and readability of non-boss messages.
  • Scanning efficiency: Boss messages stand out clearly in all three variations. The question is how much to de-emphasize non-boss messages -- too much and they become hard to read, too little and the differentiation isn't effective.
